July Shaheed Smriti Foundation

The July Shaheed Smrity Foundation has been set up to support the injured and families of those who were affected by the July revolution. With thousands injured and hundreds of lives lost, the foundation will focus on raising funds to provide immediate health care support to injured people, offer short-term financial support to victim families, preserve the memory of those who sacrificed their lives, and implement projects that will ensure long-term impact.
